I am drawn to the University of Macau because of its comprehensive Computer Science curriculum and emphasis on both theoretical foundations and practical applications. I am particularly excited by the opportunity to collaborate with peers on projects that challenge creativity and technical problem-solving, and to take advantage of the university’s computing resources and interdisciplinary environment. While my focus is on games, I value the flexibility of this program, which will allow me to explore diverse areas of computer science and apply them to interactive media and entertainment.

Conclusion

I am confident that my academic preparation, professional experiences, and creative vision make me a strong candidate for the Computer Science program at the University of Macau. I am eager to contribute to the academic community, collaborate on innovative projects, and continue my journey of advancing knowledge in computer science through the art and science of game design.
